About

Hello and welcome to my page! I'm Ummi Salma Ahmed-Sumaila, and I'm thrilled to share my journey with you. Hailing from the bustling city of Lagos, Nigeria, my story is a blend of curiosity, hard work, and a passion for technology.

Early Days and Education

Growing up in Lagos, Nigeria, my early interests were far from technology. Graduating High School at 14, I spent two years learning hand skills and computer literacy before pursuing a Computer Science degree at Crescent University, graduating with top honors.

Skills

I enjoy analyzing data using SQL, Python, and Excel, and creating visual stories with Power BI and Tableau. My design skills shine with Figma and Sketch, transforming ideas into user-friendly designs. I'm also a strong communicator and researcher.
